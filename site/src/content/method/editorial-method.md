## Editorial method

Microsoft Agentic AI Weekly is an approval-first briefing.

Automation produces candidate items and draft summaries. Human review is mandatory before publication and email send.

## Canonical story unit

Every accepted issue item is rendered as:

- Signal
- Mini-abstract
- Why click
- Source confidence

## Quality gates

Candidates are rejected when they fail one or more gates:

- Duplicate or near-duplicate coverage
- Generic or boilerplate phrasing
- Weak Microsoft or direct implementation relevance
- Excessive concentration from a single source bucket

## Source policy

- Source list changes require explicit owner approval.
- Every item must include canonical URL and publisher.
- Confidence labels must reflect source type and evidence quality.

## Corrections policy

- Corrections are logged and reviewed by a human editor.
- Material factual/link errors are corrected transparently.
- Publish/send automation never bypasses correction workflow.
