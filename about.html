Editorial Charter

Decision-useful intelligence for the Microsoft agentic stack.

Microsoft Agentic AI Weekly serves three audiences every week: builders, platform owners, and technical leaders. We prioritize implementation realities over product marketing.

What we cover

  • Copilot Studio, Power Platform, and M365 extensibility patterns
  • Azure AI model/platform shifts with practical implications
  • Security, governance, cost, ALM, and production operations
  • Independent field lessons and community implementation guidance

How each issue is structured

  • Why it matters in one line
  • Who should care (Builder / Platform Owner / Leader)
  • Decision context (concise human summary for fast evaluation)
  • Confidence level (Official / Reputable / Early signal)

Trust + provenance at a glance

  • Active machine-ingestable sources: 26
  • Candidate feeds under audit: 15+
  • Rejected/non-ingestable watchlist: explicitly tracked with reasons in data/sources.json

We removed the dedicated Sources page from primary navigation to reduce clutter. Provenance remains transparent here plus in the repository audit artifacts.

Approval-first policy (non-negotiable)

Automation prepares source queues and issue drafts. Final publish to the site and newsletter send always require explicit human approval.
